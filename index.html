The Fresh and
OrganicFruits

Fruits are the edible, fleshy, and sweet parts of plants that typically contain seeds. They are a significant part of human nutrition and are highly recommended for a healthy diet. Fruits are generally low in calories and are a great source of dietary fiber and essential vitamins. They can also act as natural laxatives due to the presence of cellulose, pectin, and various organic acids. Fruits come in a wide variety of colors, shapes, and flavors, and are usually eaten raw, though some varieties can be cooked.
